Cinnamon Twists

Description

These cinnamon puff pastry twists are filled with sweet cinnamon sugar, and made from only 6 six ingredients, making them an easy treat for any time of year.

Ingredients

  • 1 - Rectangular sheet puff pastry
  • 42g - Melted butter
  • 1/3 cup - Brown sugar
  • 3 tsps - Ground cinnamon
  • 1 - Egg yolk (with a dash of milk)
  • Cinnamon Sugar for dusting

Steps

  1. Line a large baking tray with baking paper.
  2. Combine the melted butter, brown sugar and ground cinnamon in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Lay out the cold puff pastry sheet on a clean surface
  4. With the long side closest to you, pour the cinnamon butter filling over the bottom half of the pastry. Spread from edge to edge, leaving the top half and a 1cm border closest to you bare.
  5. Fold the top half of the pastry down, towards you, over the top so the filling is now inside the pastry.
  6. Use the tines of a fork to seal the long edge closest to you.
  7. Cut the pastry into strips, about 1 Â½ cm wide.
  8. Holding the two ends, twist the pastry 4 times and place it onto the lined baking try.
  9. Chill the pastries in the freezer for 15 minutes.
  10. Preheat the oven to 200C
  11. Remove the pastries from the freezer and brush with egg wash.
  12. Place pastries in the oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
  13. Let the pastries cool and then dust with cinnamon sugar.
