Beef Ragu Recipe

Description

This beef ragu is slow cooked in a red wine reduction, resulting in rich, tender beef.

This is a great recipe to use for meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 30ml - Olive Oil
  • 1kg - Beef Chuck, Diced
  • 1 - Brown Onion, Diced
  • 1 - Head of Garlic, Crushed & Chopped
  • 3Tbsp - Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 Cup - Pinot Noir Red Wine
  • 1 - Bouillon Cube, Crushed
  • 800g - Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 Cup - Beef Stock
  • 6 - Fresh Thyme Sprigs
  • 3 - Dried Bay Leaves
  • Splash of Worcestershire Sauce
  • 500g - Wide Pasta, Tagliatelle
  • Seasoning to taste
  • Parmesan Cheese To Serve
  • Fresh Basil To Serve

Steps

  1. Dice onions, crush and dice garlic, and dice beef into chunks. Keep beef separate to garlic and onion.
  2. Add beef to a bowl and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add the Olive Oil to a high-rimmed pan or pot and pre-heat to Medium-High.
  4. Add the beef to the pan in batches and cook until brown all over. Set cooked beef aside.
  5. Add Onion the pan with a pinch of salt and sweat for approximately 4 minutes.
  6. Add Garlic to the onion and cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add Tomato Paste to the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  8. From here, add in the Red Wine and Crushed Bouillon Cube. Mix well, and reduce for approximately 2 minutes.
  9. Lastly, add in the Beef Stock, Crushed Tomatoes, Thyme Sprigs, Bay Leaves and Cooked Beef, along with the Beef resting juices.
  10. Mix well and bring to a light boil.
  11. Reduce the heat to a low, add a lid to the pan and let simmer for a minimum of 2 hours.
  12. Once complete, remove the herbs and break up the beef.
  13. Add in the cooked pasta and mix until evenly combined.
  14. Serve topped with Parmesan Cheese and Fresh Basil.
